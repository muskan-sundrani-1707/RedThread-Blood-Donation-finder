About Red Thread

Saving Lives Through Intelligent Donor Matching — Technology for Life Saving

The Problem with Current Systems

Traditional blood donation coordination relies heavily on manual processes: phone calls, WhatsApp groups, and word of mouth. During emergencies—accidents, surgeries, childbirth—every minute counts. Delays in finding a suitable donor can be life-threatening.

Delay in Emergencies

When someone needs blood urgently, families often spend hours contacting multiple people, hospitals, and blood banks. There is no quick way to know who is available, nearby, and has the right blood group. This delay can cost lives.

Importance of Fast Donor Matching

Red Thread focuses on speed and relevance. We match donors based on:

  • Blood group – Exact match required
  • Location & distance – Nearest donors first
  • Availability – Who can donate immediately
  • Urgency level – Critical requests get top priority

How ML Improves Prediction

Our machine learning model improves donor matching by analyzing:

  • Historical donation behavior – Past responsiveness and reliability
  • Age & demographics – Patterns in availability by age group
  • Location patterns – Traffic, distance, typical response times
  • Time of request – When donors are most likely available

This helps surface the most likely available donors first, reducing time to connect.

Role of Age, Location & Donation History

Demographic and behavioral features improve match quality. Age affects eligibility and availability patterns. Location determines how quickly a donor can reach a hospital. Donation history indicates reliability and willingness to help again.

Red Thread brings all of this together to help save lives when every second matters.
